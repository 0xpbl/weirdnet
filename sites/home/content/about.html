what is weirdnet?

weirdnet is a human-edited map of the weird / small web — places you don’t find through algorithmic feeds. no dashboards, no growth hacks, no engagement bait.

phase 1 ships two static islands: drops (a chronological link log) and directory (a curated catalog).

phase 2 will bring public submissions, webrings, guestbooks, and bbs experiments — slowly and carefully.
